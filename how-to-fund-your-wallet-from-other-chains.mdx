## Step-by-Step Deposit Guide

With Clave and Layerswap working together, transferring your crypto assets to zkSync Era Mainnet has never been more convenient. This powerful partnership ensures that you can deposit funds from any supported blockchain swiftly and cost-effectively. Whether you're a seasoned crypto user or just getting started, Layerswap simplifies the process, allowing you to focus on exploring new opportunities within the Clave ecosystem. From accessing decentralized apps to participating in the latest projects, your funds will be ready for use in just a few minutes, giving you more time to engage with the innovative features Clave has to offer.

### Let's See How the Deposit Works

![https://blog2.getclave.io/content/images/2024/06/Frame-1000005230.png](https://blog2.getclave.io/content/images/2024/06/Frame-1000005230.png)

1 - Open **Clave**

2- Tap **Hub** to see all actions you can do in Clave including **Deposit**.

3- Tap **Deposit** : Here, you can see all the chains you can send your funds to.

![https://blog2.getclave.io/content/images/2024/06/Frame-1000005231.png](https://blog2.getclave.io/content/images/2024/06/Frame-1000005231.png)

1. Select the network you have your crypto tokens on other wallets.
2. Select which token and how much you want to deposit.

![https://blog2.getclave.io/content/images/2024/06/Frame-1000005232.png](https://blog2.getclave.io/content/images/2024/06/Frame-1000005232.png)

1. Send tokens on the selected network to **Deposit Address**.
    1. For example, send 0.1 ETH on Optimism to the address provided.
    2. When the transfer is completed, your tokens will be deposited automatically to your Clave account.
2. Check deposit status.
    - You can check the status of your deposit in the "**Pending Deposits**" section.
    - You will receive a notification when your funds arrive in your wallet.

Now you are ready to be prepared for ZK Summer. Deposit to your Clave account and be ready!
