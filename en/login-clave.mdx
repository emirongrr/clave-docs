---
title: 'Logging Out'
description: 'Learn how to log out of the Clave app securely. Follow these steps to sign out of your account from the Clave app.'
---

If you want to log out or change your account, you can follow these steps to log out. 


1. Open the Clave app and tap on the **Profile** icon located at the bottom left of the screen.

2. Under Profile Section, tap on the **Log out** option at the bottom of the list.

3. A **confirmation pop-up** will appear asking if you are sure you want to log out. Click on the **Log out** button to confirm.

 ![Account Settings](./en/images/login2.png)
After logging out, you will be redirected to the login screen, ensuring your account is securely logged out of the device.
