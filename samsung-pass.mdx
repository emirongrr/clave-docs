**Creating an Account on Clave with Samsung Pass**

If you are using Samsung Pass on your Android device, you should first add another password account to your settings.

1. Go to **Settings -> Passwords -> Passwords and Automatic Filling -> Add Bitwarden**.
2. Open the **Clave app**.
3. Click on **“Create new account.”**
4. You will see the **“Create passkey”** pop-up.
5. Select **Samsung Pass** as your passkey option.
6. Click **continue**, and you created your Clave account!

If you have any problems with creating your Clave account, you can check our [troubleshooting](problem-solving) page.
